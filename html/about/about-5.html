SERVICES

your position: Home > Download > Borchures > SERVICES

Our high quality products and fast delivery services has been an important factor in attracting and maintaining our customers. We place great emphasis on quality assurance and we have in place a stringent quality control procedure, and manual which we require all our staff to comply with throughout the design process. We monitor our design process closely and conduct stringent quality assurance checks, which include quality assurance at the design stage, incoming quality assurance of components and parts, assembly testing and final quality testing.

Our selection of suppliers for components and parts, as well as for the materials used in the mass production of USB Flash,and LED screem for our customer is based on the supplier‘s industry reputation, whether it has been certified as having met applicable industry standards for quality assurance, market share and product reliability. All incoming parts, components and materials used in the production process are first forwarded to our quality assurance department for quality control checks by trained quality assurance personnel. The quality control checks include dimensional checks, visual checks and functional checks to ensure that the parts and components conform to required specifications. We also subject certain of our components such as chipsets, as well as any prototype photo frames developed by us to stringent and comprehensive testing. We are equipped with accredited lab with full scale test capabilities, such as RF Test Studio ,Audio Test Studio, Current Test Studio, EMC Test Studio, Network Analysis Test Studio.

During the production process, we undertake sample testing of the PCB mainboards, to ensure the quality of our flash chipsets. We will also assist our customers to ensure that quality assurance standards in the mass production of digital Photo Frames & USB drives are complied with.
