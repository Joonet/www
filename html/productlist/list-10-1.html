We make custom mould of both thermoplastic and thermosetting parts. We also produce the plastic pasts with our injection machines. Our experiences of mold development and part injection ranges from consumer products to household machines to industrial equipments.
In the last decades, we worked with our customers developed molds such as MP3 players, Digital Cameras, Digital photo Frames, Cell phones, Computer Monitors, Wasing Machines, Ice Boxes , Automobile Parts...
Our mold designers and mold makers are in a passion to being every single mold to be perfect, while offer our customers the lowest possible price.
